Shiro Wat (Ethiopian Chickpea Stew)

Description

Nothing really hits like a well timed bowl of Cereal. If you are anything like me you can eat truck loads of the stuff. Here is how I like to prepare my Cereal!

  • Cereal
  • Cereal Bowl
  • Milk or milk alternative
  • Spoon
  1. Grab a box of cereal. reach for whichever one speaks out to you! No need to worry about calories of any of that stuff (unless you have a medical condition). I prefer Chocolate Frosted Flakes.
  2. Pour out the cereal into your bowl. I like to leave about 1/8 of rim so the milk doesn't cause it to spill over.
  3. Next, grab your favorite milk or milk alternative. Begin to pour untill the cereal rises to the brim of the bowl.
  4. Lastly. gently place the spoon into the bowl.
  5. Enjoy!
